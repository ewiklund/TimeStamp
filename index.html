Timestamp-api for FCC

Directions:

1. Append a date or a unix timestamp to the end of the url for this Heroku app ("https://cryptic-oasis-58746.herokuapp.com/November%2019,%115415" or "https://cryptic-oasis-58746.herokuapp.com/1450137600" and the app will return the Unix timestamp and the natural language form of that date.

This is what you should see:

{ unix: 1450137600, natural: "November 26, 2019" }
