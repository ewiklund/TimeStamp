Timestamp-api for FCC

Directions:

1. Append a date or a unix timestamp to the end of the url for this Heroku app and it will return the Unix timestamp and the natural language form of that date.

Example Usage:

  • [project url]/api/timestamp/2019-11-27
  • [project url]/api/timestamp/1574848131

This is what you should see:

{"unix": 1574848131, "utc":"Wed, 27 November 2019 09:50:00 GMT"}
